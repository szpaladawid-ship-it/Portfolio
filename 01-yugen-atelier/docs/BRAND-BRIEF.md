# Yūgen Atelier — Brand Brief

## Concept

Yūgen Atelier is a fictional architecture and spatial design practice operating between Tokyo and Oslo. The studio creates calm, durable spaces shaped by light, material, silence, and human movement.

## Positioning

A contemporary architecture studio for clients seeking restrained, culturally aware and highly considered spaces.

## Audience

- private residential clients,
- boutique hospitality brands,
- cultural institutions,
- premium retail concepts,
- developers seeking distinctive small-scale projects.

## Brand values

- precision,
- restraint,
- material honesty,
- cultural sensitivity,
- longevity,
- clarity.

## Tone of voice

### English

Measured, precise and visual. Avoid exaggerated claims and sales language.

### Japanese

Polite, clear and information-rich. Important project facts should remain easy to verify.

## Visual direction

- generous spacing,
- editorial composition,
- large architectural imagery,
- asymmetric but controlled grids,
- neutral materials and warm grey surfaces,
- one restrained accent,
- subtle motion rather than spectacle.

## Suggested palette

- Ink: `#111111`
- Paper: `#F3F0EA`
- Stone: `#C9C3B8`
- Mist: `#E5E2DC`
- Copper accent: `#9B6B4A`

## Typography direction

### Latin

A refined serif for display text paired with a neutral sans-serif for navigation and metadata.

### Japanese

Use a high-quality Japanese Gothic or Mincho family and test real Japanese content from the start.

Suggested fallback stack:

```css
font-family: "Noto Sans JP", "Hiragino Kaku Gothic ProN", "Yu Gothic", sans-serif;
```

## Motion principles

- slow reveal,
- image masking,
- subtle parallax,
- quiet hover feedback,
- no forced scroll,
- no long intro,
- no decorative animation that blocks content.
