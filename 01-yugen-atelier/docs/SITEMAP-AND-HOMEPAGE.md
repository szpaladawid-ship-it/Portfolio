## Homepage motion plan

### Hero

- full-bleed architectural image,
- slow image scale from 1.04 to 1.00,
- headline reveal by line,
- subtle vertical metadata movement,
- no autoplay video in version 1.

### Selected projects

- image mask reveal on scroll,
- quiet title transition on hover,
- category and location remain visible without hover.

### Philosophy section

- one restrained Lottie or SVG line animation inspired by architectural drawing,
- animation must remain optional and lightweight.

### Language switch

- visible `EN / 日本語` control,
- no flag icons,
- preserve the current page when changing language.
